What is a GPA Calculator?

A GPA calculator is a tool used to calculate your grade point average based on the grades you enter.

How is GPA calculated?

The formula used to calculate GPA is quite simple. For each course, the course hours and course grade are multiplied together. This product is added to the total grade points. The sum of all the course hours is the total course credits. The GPA is the total grade points is divided by the total course credits.
